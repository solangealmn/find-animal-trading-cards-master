Clownfish

A layer of mucus on the clownfish's skins makes it immune to the fish-eating anemone's lethal sting

  • Scientific Name: Amphiprioninae
  • Average Length: 11 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 7 years
  • Habitat: Tropical coral reefs

Bright orange with three distinctive white bars, clown anemonefish are among the most recognizable of all reef-dwellers. They reach about 11 centimeters in lenght, and named for the multicolered sea anemone in the which they makes their homes.
